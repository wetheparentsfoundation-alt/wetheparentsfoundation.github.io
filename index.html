What we do

Court Watching

Trained volunteers attend hearings (in‑person or virtual), observe quietly, and submit standardized reports to document systemic patterns.

Education

Plain‑language guides on due process, equal protection, speech, and search/seizure boundaries as applied to family court.

Community

Peer support, templates, and resource referrals so parents aren’t navigating the process alone.
