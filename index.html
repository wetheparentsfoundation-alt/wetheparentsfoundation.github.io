Equal Rights. Shared Light.

Turning pain into purpose.

We The Parents Foundation brings sunlight to family courts through respectful court watching, education, and community support—so every family can access fairness and due process.

Get Court Watch Support About the Foundation

Court Watching

Trained volunteers attend hearings (in-person or virtual), observe quietly, and submit standardized reports to document patterns.

Education

Plain-language guides on due process, equal protection, speech, and search/seizure boundaries in family court.

Community

Peer support, templates, and resource referrals so parents aren’t navigating the process alone.

Sunlight and documentation drive change.

We observe, we don’t disrupt. Privacy and safety come first. Your lived experience can help improve outcomes for every family.
