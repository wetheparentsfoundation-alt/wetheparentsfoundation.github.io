About the Foundation

We are a parent‑led, non‑partisan effort focused on fairness, transparency, and child‑centered outcomes in family courts. We build tools and community so families can assert their rights respectfully and effectively.

Principles

  • Every family deserves due process and equal protection.
  • We observe, we don’t disrupt. Sunlight and documentation drive change.
  • Evidence over rumor. Privacy and safety come first.
